Module 4 — Separator Sizing

  1. Gas flow rate at operationg conditions
    $$ Q_{g(oper)} = Q_{g(std)} \times \frac{P_{std}}{P_{abs}} \times \frac{T_{oper}}{T_{std}} $$
    $$ Q_{g(oper)} = (0.86) \times \left( \frac{14.7}{290} \right) \times \left( \frac{573}{520} \right) = 0.0473 \text{ MMSCFD} = 0.547 \text{ ft}^3/s $$
  2. Maximum allowable gas velocity (Souders-Brown)
    2.1 Gas Density
    $$ \rho_g = 2.7 \times \frac{SG \times P_{op}}{T_{op} \times Z} \left( \frac{\text{lb}}{\text{ft}^3} \right) $$
    $$ \rho_g = 2.7 \times \frac{0.6 \times 290}{573 \times 0.92} = 0.89 \left( \frac{\text{lb}}{\text{ft}^3} \right) $$
    2.2 Liquid Density
    $$ \rho_l = 62.4 \times \frac{141.5}{131.5 + API} \left( \frac{\text{lb}}{\text{ft}^3} \right) $$
    $$ \rho_l = 62.4 \times \frac{141.5}{131.5 + 24} = 56.8 \left( \frac{\text{lb}}{\text{ft}^3} \right) $$
    2.3 Gas Velocity (Sounders-Brown)
    $$ V_{gas,max} = K_{SB} \sqrt{\frac{\rho_L - \rho_g}{\rho_g}} $$
    $$ V_{\mathrm{gas,max}} = 0.197 \sqrt{\frac{56,8 - 0.89}{0.89}} = 1,56 \, \mathrm{ft/s} $$
  3. Minimum droplet size
    $$ V_t = \frac{d_p^2 (\rho_l - \rho_g) g}{18\mu_g} $$
    $$ V_{\mathrm{t,min}} = \frac{v_{\mathrm{gas}}}{\frac{L}{D}} = \frac{1.56}{3} = 0.52 \, \frac{\mathrm{ft}}{\mathrm{s}} $$
    $$ d_{\mathrm{p,min}} = \sqrt{\frac{18\mu_g \, v_{\mathrm{t,min}}}{(\rho_l - \rho_g)g}} $$
    $$ d_{\mathrm{p,min}} = \sqrt{\frac{18 \times 8.06 \times 10^{-6} \times 0.52}{(56.8 - 0.89) \times 32.2}} = 2 \times 10^{-4} \mathrm{ft} $$
    $$ d_{\mathrm{p,min}} \approx 7.8 \times 10^{-5} \mathrm{m} \approx 78 \mathrm{\mu m} $$
  4. Liquid residence time
    According to the GPSA Engineering Data standard Book for high viscosity oil:
    $$ \tau_{\mathrm{ret}} = 15 \, \mathrm{min} = 900 \, \mathrm{sec} $$
    $$ V_L = Q_l \times \tau_{\mathrm{ret}} = \left( \frac{28300 \times 5.615}{86400} \right) \times 900 = 1656 \, \mathrm{ft}^3 $$
  5. Separator dimensions
    Assuming liquid occupies 50% of the cross-section
    $$ A_L = 1.178 \, D^2 $$
    For L/D = 3
    $$ V_L = A_l \times L = 1.178 D^3 $$
    $$ D^3 = \frac{1656}{1.178} = 11.2 \, \mathrm{ft} $$
    Accepted standard diameter: Dsep = 3.6 m
    $$ L_{\mathrm{sep}} = \frac{V}{0.3927 \times D^2} $$
    $$ L_{\mathrm{sep}} = \frac{1656}{0.3927 \times 11.2^2} = 33.7 \, \mathrm{ft} $$
    $$ L_{\mathrm{tt}} = 33.7 \, \mathrm{ft} $$
  6. Verification
    Area
    $$ A_g = 0.5 \times \frac{\pi D^2}{4} $$
    $$ A_g = 0.5 \times \frac{3.14 \times 11.2^2}{4} = 50.5 \, \mathrm{ft}^2 $$
    Gas Velocity
    $$ v_{\mathrm{g,act}} = \frac{Q_g}{A} $$
    $$ v_{\mathrm{g,act}} = \frac{0.168}{50.5} = 0.0033 \, \frac{\mathrm{ft}}{\mathrm{s}} \ll 2.46 \, \frac{\mathrm{ft}}{\mathrm{s}} $$
    $$ v_{\mathrm{g,act}} \ll v_{\mathrm{gas,max}} = 0.033 \, \frac{\mathrm{ft}}{\mathrm{s}} $$
    $$ \tau_{\mathrm{act}} = 17 \, \mathrm{min} \geq 15 \, \mathrm{min} $$
    $$ \frac{L}{D} = 2.9 \, (\mathrm{range} \, 2 - 4) $$
